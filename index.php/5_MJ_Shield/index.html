Shields protect space vessels from damage in space.
Primarily intended to protect against micrometeorite erosion, shields also protect against collision and weapons fire.

Shields are broadly broken down into three categories - Light, Medium and Heavy

Shield Types[edit]

Light Shields[edit]

Most often found on M5 Scout and M4 Interceptor ships, these shields tend to be able to stand up to only the lightest of weapons fire.

Medium Shields[edit]

Medium Shields are most often found on M3 Fighter and M6 Corvette military ships, and most non-military ships; although occasionally also on M4 Interceptor ships too.
