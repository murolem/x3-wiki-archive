Overview

The BoFu Chemical Lab manufactures Boron BoFu, an important raw material for the Boron economy, from Energy Cells and BoGas.

NPC factories take Plankton and Soja Husk as secondary resources.

Encyclopedia Entry

A BoFu Chemical Lab manufactures BoFu meals. The BoFu is then transported to the various Boron Space stations and other Boron factories where it is the main food product.

BoFu is produced from BoGas, Plankton, and other secret ingredients. Even though the Boron are famous for their delicious Stott spices among other races, the taste of BoFu is no joy to anybody but them.

Availability

This factory is unique to the Boron.
