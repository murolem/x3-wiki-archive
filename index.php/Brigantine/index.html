Overview

The Brigantine equips a good range of weapons but is handicapped by its limited forward weapon power and poor defensive shielding; thus, it is the weakest of M2s for players. Nevertheless it is a popular ship, often used as boarding practice.

Background

The worst fears of shipping and trading companies were recently realised when the existence of a pirate destroyer was confirmed. Military forces have increased their vigilance in the face of this new threat.

Physical Characteristics

Combat

The Brigantine can mount several flak cannons, making it particularly lethal against fighters. However it is weak against other capital ships due to its poor shielding and less than ideal anti-capital turret arcs. It has average speed for a destroyer so it cannot easily retreat from a fight, but with a competent pilot it is a force to be reckoned with.

Suggested Roles

Light patrol duty; the ship cannot defend itself out of sector from enemy M7s or greater.
