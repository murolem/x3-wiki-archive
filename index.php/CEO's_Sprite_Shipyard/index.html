[edit] Description

Highly destructive PAC/PPC Weapons are built here under strict Teladi Military guard. Most of the personnel in this system are tied to the military arm of the Profit Guild or the smaller Teladi Company Navy. The market for recreational and luxury goods is high among the personnel on guard detail in a relatively safe system.
