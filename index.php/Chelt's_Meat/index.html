Overview

Encyclopedia Entry

Chelts are marine animals that once roamed the seas and oceans on the Split home planet that the Split killed for meat, oil and their skins, which they processed to create a tough leather-like material. However for many years Chelts were over-hunted and almost brought to extinction. Nowadays Chelts are grown in space Chelt Aquariums and are later sold to Split food factories.

Commentary

The Chelt is one of the three surviving species of Hodie, the original homeworld of the Split, the others are the Split (Obviously) and the Scruffin plant.
