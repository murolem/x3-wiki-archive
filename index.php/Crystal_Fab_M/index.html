Overview

Crystal Fabs produce Crystals from Energy Cells, Silicon Wafers and Food.

Encyclopedia Entry

Crystal Fabs produce many crystals that are used in the manufacturing processes of Particle Accelerators found in many Laser Factories. Crystals are produced from silicon wafers.

The production process involves the melting and refining of the silicone using BoGas. This mixture is then cooled and various elements added to produce different grades and types of crystal.
