Overview[edit]

Crystals are important for the production of various weapons. They are produced in Crystal Fab facilities throughout the universe using silicon wafers.

Commentary[edit]

This is a fairly high demand ware in the X-universe. All Solar Power Plants use crystals to create energy cells. Almost every trading port will buy them.

While NPC solar power plants list crystals as a feed stock, they do not consume them normally, and actually produce power regardless of their crystal levels.
