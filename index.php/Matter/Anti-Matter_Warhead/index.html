Encyclopedia Entry

Matter/Anti-matter Warheads contain an equal amount of matter and anti-matter separated by an energy field. When this field is compromised, by impact or failure, the two mix with devastating results.

Weapons

Used by both the Matter/Anti-Matter Launcher and the Prototype Matter/Anti-Matter Launcher.
