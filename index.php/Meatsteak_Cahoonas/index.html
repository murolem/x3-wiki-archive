Overview[edit]

Argon meatsteaks are traded throughout the universe. They provide a nutritious meat based food suitable for most types of races. Some races prefer to use Argon meatsteaks in conjuncture with different Boron spices. It is traded in blocks known as Cahoonas.

Commentary[edit]

This product is produced at the Argon Cahoona Bakery M and Cahoona Bakery L Cahoonas are the Argon's main food product. because of this they are required by many Argon factories as a primary resource.
