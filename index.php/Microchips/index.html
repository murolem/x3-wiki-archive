Overview[edit]

Encyclopedia Entry[edit]

Microchips are manufactured from silicon wafers that are especially treated with various coatings to create the required conductivity. They are mainly used in the production process of computer components.

Production[edit]

Microchips are made at a Chip Plant. They require Silicon Wafers, Energy Cells and Food.

Commentary[edit]

The Hub plot requires 75500 Microchips. It is wise to set up a production facility for them, preferably near a Silicon Mine.
