Overview[edit]

Exactly the same as the Aran in all but name and the fact that this ship is not anywhere in a vanilla Universe, but the Aran is. Somewhere...

Background[edit]

Physical Characteristics[edit]

Exactly the same as the Aran. Psychedelic, christmas tree-esque yellow lighting and massive bulk included.

Combat[edit]

Same as the Aran. Big ole mothership/just about mobile storage unit. Completely unarmed, not even missiles. It's the fact that it can lug around a destroyer/carrier/frigate and thirty corvettes that makes it deadly.

Equipment[edit]

Availability[edit]

The Mobile Repair Ship is not available for sale.

Just like the Aran - Big ole mothership/Barely-mobile-storage-unit.
