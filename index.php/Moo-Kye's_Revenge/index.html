Description

This rogue sector is on the main trade line lane between old sectors and more recent expensions of the Paranid Empire. A small trading post that runs itself independently from other races. It was little or no police, with pirate raiding parties taking the opportunity to seize power in this system. Most ships that come though this sector can be expected to come under some form of attack, and with the Paranid not willing to send in forces to assist ships with the exception of other Paranid traders, there is little chance of official help.
