Description

This sector was once the furthest Paranid system from Paranid Prime and it held that little for several decades. Despite the Xaarist Priests ran Preacher's Refuge with strict religious fervour and property. Their quiet and devoted worker-monks produced exquisite jewellery, inspiration 'Xja' poetry and the very best in automated, semi-intelligent weapons. In recent times, the focus has shifted to sophisticated technology production and interspecies trade has boomed, but the religious extremism remains, hidden in the shadows.
