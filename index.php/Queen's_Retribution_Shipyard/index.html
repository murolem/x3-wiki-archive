Description

In striking out in the growing conflict with the Split, Menelaus established this sector to be the first in the new colonies. A Core sector, specialising in technological and military output, hell hath no fury greater than a Queen's retribution.


Shipyard Availability

A Boron Super Shipyard close to Split sectors.
