Reputation

From X3 Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

See Also: Ranks

Reputation is a reflection of how a faction views you. Sufficiently negative reputation means that the faction in question considers you an enemy, will attempt to kill you on sight, and are usually marked with red reticules and sector map markers for your visual convenience.

Combat and Trade Ranks can be found Here.

At a race rank of -1 you may dock with a race's stations in border sectors and at a rank of 0 in core sectors.

Each rank requires roughly 3 times the points to advance. For each rank you gain, you must do 3 times the work, or do things worth 3 times the notability. Each step being (more or less) geometrically harder then the last.

An "L" displayed in-game after the race's rank indicated the player owns a Police License for that faction.
