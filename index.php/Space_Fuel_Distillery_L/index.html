Overview

This station produces Space Fuel from Energy Cells and Delexian Wheat.

NPC stations buy Stott Spices as secondary resources.

Encyclopedia Entry

This secret factory is a distillery that produces Argon Whisky, better known to all pilots as Space Fuel.

The whisky is produced from the husks of Delexian Wheat, which are mashed and mixed with Boron Spices. Due to recurring difficulties with getting supplies of Wheat through Argon Police patrols the manufacturers use Soja husk as a substitute.

Availability

This factory is unique to the Argon.
