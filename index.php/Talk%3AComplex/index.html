OOS Trading vs. IS Trading[edit]

It's too late for me to be editing the main page, but it should probably be noted that if you stay IS with your complex, then it's more likely other ships will crash into it etc. Effectively, if you're going to SETA for long periods and have freighters coming and going at all times, do it in another system (that is also relatively safe, and well away from the ecliptic), that way your freighters don't crash into jump gates or stray TLs. Korlus (talk) 23:13, 19 March 2013 (CDT)
