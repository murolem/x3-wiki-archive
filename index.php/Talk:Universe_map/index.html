I am currently working on a PDF (vector-based) rendition of the X3TC universe map. I intend to include race information, directionality, stations in each sector, asteroids and a place for some notes (should you want to place where your player stations are located) for those that have a PDF editing program (which can later be made into an image map, but it is great to be able to zoom in and read all the information). Filling out all the stations in every sector is a daunting task, so anyone who wants to help by providing me with information on sector contents are welcomed to message me here or email me.

I started mapping this as I played my Terran game, so I have much of the Terran contents already recorded, but not all of them. I also have some of the sectors around the Terran territory mapped. I'll work on getting a list of the sectors already accounted for. The main idea here is to take a snapshot of the universe at start up. The game dynamically builds stations as your play progresses. Some stations may be destroyed, and others may be built. I want to capture what is there at the very beginning.

--Whitehorn 02/02/2012

quick tutorial on template

{{mapcell|Kingdom End|Boron|0|1|1|0}}

is an example call.
mapcell is the template.
Kingdom End is the sector name
Boron is the owning faction.
|0|1|1|0 refers to top, right, bottom, left. or north, east, south, west, the 1 means that there's a gate on that side, a 0 means there isn't. If you don't know, there's a copy of the html from rohrbecka's map below, which you can use for reference, although it's pretty hard to read. I'd suggest using the linked materials.
