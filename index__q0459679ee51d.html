Overview

The most heavily shielded ship in the game, the Hive Queen boasts a colossal 18GJ of shielding, more than many stations. Otherwise, it is identical to a standard Kha'ak Carrier, right down to the bugged docking bay. This small model provides even better survivability, making the Queen a tough nut to crack, though it poses little offensive threat to opposing battleships due to its poor weaponry. It only appears in one plot related sector and is unique.

Background

Physical Characteristics

Identical to the Kha'ak Carrier.

Combat

Hard to kill, particularly since it's most likely being defended by several other battleships and many fighters. It is highly vulnerable alone, however, as its weaponry is far from impressive. Most carriers, destroyers or even frigates should have little trouble defeating it, particularly if they use the PPC or other weapons which outrange the Gamma Kyon Emitter. Even the Skirnir missile frigate can still annihilate it with a single barrage of its most powerful weapon, the Shadow Missile.

Equipment

Availability

Absolutely impossible to obtain without cheats, as all Kha'ak battleships are unboardable and are not sold anywhere.

Suggested Roles

If you do cheat one in, it would make a perfect command ship in combat due to its survivability and inherently good fighter defence.
