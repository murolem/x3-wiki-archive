[edit] Overview

The Cutlass is a very powerful fighter, weighing in as one of the most capable ships short of the Corvette class. It does not suffer the speed penalty that shortfalls others in the M3+ class, making it a very dangerous all-purpose fighter. However, since only Terran weapons equip on it, it is very difficult to arm.

[edit] Physical Characteristics

[edit] Combat

A fully-armed Cutlass is a shockingly efficient killer. Nothing short of a Corvette, or a fighter with a particularly brutal front weapon loadout such as the Spitfyre, will trouble it in combat. You can trust an automated Cutlass to come off best in almost any dogfight.

It is also one of the very few fighters that can claim to be useful against Kha'ak beam weapons - its shielding is sufficient to keep it alive while its firepower dismantles enemy craft.

[edit] Suggested Roles

Carrier fighter par excellence. Its survivability and attack power mean it can defeat most foes, so if enough EMPCs can be gathered for a Cutlass wing, it makes for one of the top choices for AI controlled fighter.
