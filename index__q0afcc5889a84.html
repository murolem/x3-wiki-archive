Fighters are the small craft of the X Universe.

They are nearly universally dockable at any location that has docking systems.

Fighters are small ships including the M5 Scout, M4 Interceptor, and M3 Fighter.

The page links below use a sortkey:

Creating a progression from E-S of decreasing weight.
