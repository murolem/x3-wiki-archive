Overview[edit]

Satellite Factories produce Navigation Relay Satellites from Energy Cells, Majaglit and Silicon Wafers.

NPC stations will buy Quantum Tubes as a secondary resource.

Encyclopedia Entry[edit]

This stations manufactures Navigation relay Satellites. With the invention of sending information through the gate network, satellites were developed to assist in boosting these transmissions, scientists later added a small sensor network to the satellites to assist in navigation and long range managing of stations.

The factory uses delicate machinery to build these complicated small devices.
