The M7M missile frigate is a class of ship armed primarily with missiles.
Specifically, these missiles are the:

  • Hammer Heavy Torpedo anti-capital missile
  • Flail Barrage Missile, a swarm anti-fighter missile
  • Boarding pods for Marine boarding operations.

ATF Skirnirs are equipped with the Shadow Missile and Ghoul Missile. They cannot equip boarding pods. The OTAS Sirokos is a special M7M intended for boarding, and can only mount boarding pods. The OTAS Aquilo, like the Skirnir, cannot mount pods at all.

Although some have guns, these are for point defence and generally can only be effectively used against missiles and small fighters.

The M7M class ships are;

  • Argon Minotaur
  • Boron Kraken
  • Paranid Ares
  • Split Cobra
  • Teladi Gannet
  • ATF Skirnir
  • OTAS Aquilo
  • OTAS Sirokos
