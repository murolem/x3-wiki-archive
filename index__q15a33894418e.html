from faq: http://www.egosoft.com/support/faq/faq_answer_en.php?answer=932&version=9&sid=93885ee3971de23179e95efa0b6dea17

The following weapons require ammunition: Energy Bolt Chaingun - Energy Bolt Chaingun Ammunition (1S cargo per unit)

Gauss Cannon - Gauss Cannon Ammunition (25L cargo per unit)

(Prototype) Matter/Anti-Matter Launcher - Matter/Anti-matter Warhead (4S cargo per unit)

Mass Driver - Mass Driver Ammunition (1S cargo per unit)

One unit of ammunition provides 200 rounds, and is automatically removed from your cargo bay when your guns need loading.

Note that if you have multiple guns firing then ammunition will be used up more quickly, e.g. if you have 8 Gauss Cannons firing together they will get 25 shots each from a single unit of ammunition. Madned 01:02, 5 August 2010 (UTC)
