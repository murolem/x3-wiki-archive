[edit] Overview

The fastest M6 in the known universe, the Aldrin-designed Springblossom also mounts very heavy weaponry and comes at a knock-down price. It is arguably one of the two most popular corvettes in the game (Hyperion is the other).

[edit] Background

The Springblossom class was originally a derivation of the Winterblossom design used to first explore the gate network. This version seems to be modified for other uses besides exploration.

[edit] Physical Characteristics

The Springblossom resembles a bullet with four rear-sweeping fins at the points of a square. It is light gray with red trim, and bears some Aldrin decals.

[edit] Combat

The Springblossom is quick enough to strike an enemy and withdraw without the opponent's turrets ever even being brought to bear. By this point, it is too late for most opponents, and nearly too late for the rest.

Its main guns are very difficult to bring to bear against smaller targets such as fighters because they are spaced so far apart that firing at the targetting reticle will only result in the shots going around the target on both sides. However, the left and right turrets can fire forward somewhat above the boresight and, once equipped with the Prototype Starburst Shockwave Cannon, even vanilla turret AI can swat fighters effortlessly. This, combined with the Springblossom's unusually high speed, allows it hunt down and exterminate fighters like no other ship in the game (and with the Turbo Booster from the bonus pack, there is no escape even for the fastest of typically-encountered M5 scouts).

Against larger targets, the spacious cargo bay can facilitate the ammo-based Prototype Matter/Anti-Matter Launcher for the Springblossom's main guns with plenty of room left for missile (Spectre Missile or Poltergeist Missile only), drone, or even lasertower deployment. This allows the Springblossom to potentially engage and destroy any enemy in the game, but anything larger than another M6 will take time and require caution as the Springblossom's shields will fold quickly under capital-ship weaponry (and accidentally running into a Tomahawk Heavy Missile or like munition usually spells instant death).

[edit] Equipment

In 14 guns across an 8-gun main and 3 turrets, the Springblossom can mount only the unique Aldrin weaponry - the EEMPC, PM/AML, or PSSC, though equally in any mount. Has an unusually large rear turret (4-gun).

[edit] Availability

The Springblossom is available at either of the two Aldrin Ship Production Bases in Aldrin orbit.

[edit] Suggested Roles

Good as a player ship, the Springblossom's speed and fighter-destroying prowess make it the perfect choice for a rapid response unit, jumping into sectors where your assets are under attack and quickly neutralizing every threat in the Sector.

The Springblossom is also an excellent taxi, especially with with the bonus pack afterburner. This is an ideal way to raise your Terran rank, as speed is vastly more useful than anything else in Terran space, and the Springblossom has the cargo space to handle large taxi runs and still run the afterburner.

A fair choice for moderate OoS patrol if equipped with PSSC all around (as energy drain doesn't matter), though somewhat light on shielding.
