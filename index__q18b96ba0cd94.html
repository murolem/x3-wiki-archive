[edit] Overview

The Incendiary Bomb Launcher, or IBL, is a Pirate capital-ship weapon.

[edit] Encyclopedia Entry

A deadly weapon found on frigates, destroyers and carriers.

Unable to easily gain possession of high-tech capital ship weapons, pirates designed this as an alternative. The Incendiary Bomb Launcher essentially produces a large ball of fiery plasma, which it then hurls at a target.

[edit] Commentary

The IBL is manufactured only by pirates and is weaker than the other capital ship weapons, the PPC, Gauss Cannon, Ion Cannon and Terran PSP. However, since it can be mounted on many frigates it is one of the more commonly used as it outclasses all other Frigate-class weapons, and players usually provide a good deal of economic assistance to the few Incendiary Bomb Launcher Forges that they are able to hack in Pirate space, often building entire complexes nearby to feed them a continual supply of resources. As it is a Pirate station, the IBL Forge is not available at any shipyards and cannot be built by the player, making the weapon relatively rare.

This weapon may also be be randomly found in the cargo hold of captured pirate capital ships, so a player equipped for boarding operations may use this as a source of IBLs.

[edit] Known Compatible Ships

[edit] Available at Known Docks

Incendiary Bomb Launchers are sold at Incendiary Bomb Launcher Forge, a pirate station.

To acquire an IBL you need good relations with Pirates, hack an IBL forge, capture it from ships carrying IBLs, or capture a TL delivering an IBL forge.
