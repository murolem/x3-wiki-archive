Overview[edit]

Massom is a powdered form of Scruffin. It is a basic Yellow powder produced by the Split from the Scruffin, a sweet Potato like food grown by the Split. This powder is traded by other races that use it to mix and enhance their own foodstuff. It is manufactured in Massom Mills found everywhere in Split territory and sold in large, one tonne bulk containers.

Commentary[edit]

The only stations that purchase this ware are Split Trading Ports.
