Overview

The Shipyards of the Terrans are where all Terran ships and stations are crafted.

Terran Shipyards were built in response to the growing demands placed on the Earth Torus by the growing USC and ATF fleets. Much of the Terran concern for fleet maintenance now falls on these colossal space stations, which double as homes for those many Terrans who do not rank highly enough to access the homeworld.

The Terrans have recently come into contact with the lost Aldrin colony. While they are nominally Terran and share a common technological root, the Aldrin cluster feature their own technology and shipbuilding methods housed in their Aldrin Ship Production Bases.

Encyclopedia Entry

This shipyard serves as a USC ship-production and repair facility. The majority of the Terran fleet used to be produced at the Torus station around Earth. In recent years increased security in the Earth sector and a huge rise in ship demand led to the building of this station.
