[edit] Overview

The Hammerhead missile is a rare, unbuyable, and extraordinarily dangerous weapon, which can vaporize anything smaller than a Frigate.

[edit] Commentary

Big badda BOOM!

The Hammerhead is an unbuyable missile similar to the firestorm, though slightly more powerful and faster. It is commonly found on race military M3s, despite the Firestorm Torpedo being compatible only with the M6 and higher classes. It is another source of annoyance to fighter pilots, as, like the firestorm, it has a large blast radius and is powerful enough to one hit kill small ships - even any corvette except the ATF vali. Its damage is 1.2 gigajoules, so is extremely dangerous even for battleships. Players often collect them after combat with the Xenon, who also love to put them in the Q.

As with the use of all nuclear based warheads - extreme caution is strongly adviced. Premature detonation can be caused by any sort of impact in space and may lead to immediate user atomization if you have insufficient shielding installed.

A little known story/urban myth on the misuse or misunderstood use of this high level combat weapon is when a small time mining prospector decided he wanted to mine a particular asteriod by firing a hammerhead missile at it from his ship. A police report filed after a passing ship reported in a massive explosion in the sector mentioned: all that remained were various small rock renments, a part of some unidentifiable ship debris and very high levels of radiation - conclusion: inconclusive due to poor levels of evidence and remains however it could be possible proof of gross mishandling of nuclear devices.
