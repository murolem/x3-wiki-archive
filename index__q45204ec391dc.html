Overview

The Mistral is an advanced transport, incorporating the latest cutting-edge OTAS design. Not only is it the fastest freighter available, it is also the largest of the standard variants, and the most heavily armed and shielded. The Mistral freighter provides essential logistical support to the OTAS fleet and all of its activities.

A TS+ variant exists called the Mistral Super Freighter, which trades speed and armament in exchange for a much larger cargo bay and stronger shielding.

Combat

If you had to take a TS into combat, the Mistral would be your choice. With turrets that cover it on all sides, then when fully equipped it is a match for any fighter. In the hands of a capable pilot, it can take on and beat an M3+.
