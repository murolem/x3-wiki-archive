Overview

Rastar is an oil produced from the animal fat of the Chelt, A farmed Sea bound animal of the Split. This oil, once refined, is very fine and is used by most races to lubricate engines and other moving mechanical parts. It is refined in Rastar Refineries, and sold in large barrels known as Gretts.

Commentary

This is a product of the Rastar Refinery
