General

Outwardly a non-governmental security organization, the activities of this outfit suggests that they don't always subscribe to Paranid ideologies and ethics.

The in-game description of their headquarters describes them as "corrupt, sly, and manipulative Paranids bent on using every unconventional method available to acquire wealth and undermine Paranid government".

Duke's Dangerous Materials Transporters are a common sight around the X-Universe and are notorious for being a source of racial reputation and free ships.
