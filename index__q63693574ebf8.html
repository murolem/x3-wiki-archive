X3: Terran Conflict has a grand total of nineteen different starting scenarios, each of which starts you off in a different sector and with a different ship. Five are available when the game is first installed, and the others are unlocked as the player meets various prerequisites.

This page lists all the starting scenarios as of X3:TC version 3.1.1. Notes on special conditions at the beginning are included as necessary.

Key[edit]

Sector
The sector(s) the player enters the game in after the opening cinematic.
Ship
The Ships|ship(s) the player starts out owning.
Weapons
The guns the starting ship is equipped with.
Credits
The amount of money the player starts with
Prerequisites
What the player must do to unlock this gamestart.
Storyline
Which plots are available. All gamestarts except Terran Defender, USC Commander, and Aldran Adventurer require a bridging mission to begin the Terran plot.

Terran Defender[edit]

Sector
Uranus
Ship
Terran M4 Sabre
Weapons
2 Electro-Magnetic Plasma Cannons
Credits
1,000 Cr
Prerequisites
None
Storyline
All

This is arguably the most commonly chosen gamestart, particularly for new players. It puts the player into the Terran plot without requiring a bridging mission.

Tormented Teladi[edit]

Sector
Sanctity of Corruption, Tears of Greed, or Acquisition Repository
Ship
Teladi M5 Harrier Hauler
Weapons
2 Impulse Ray Emitters
Credits
1,960 Cr
Prerequisites
Trade rank 15 "Petty Financier"
Storyline
All

This gamestart allows the player to obtain an overtuned Enhanced Kea.

Poisoned Paranid[edit]

Sector
Preacher's Refuge or Heaven's Assertion
Ship
Paranid TS Demeter Hauler
Weapons
1 IRE
Credits
9,268 Cr
Prerequisites
Complete the mission at the beginning of the Tormented Teladi gamestart
Storyline
All

This gamestart gives the player a deadline to obtain several million credits. If he is successful, he gets an overtuned Hyperion Vanguard.

Unholy Traitor[edit]

Sector
Random between several sectors
Ship
Paranid M3+ Medusa Prototype
Weapons
2 PACs, 6 Pulsed Beam Emitters
Credits
21,331 Cr
Prerequisites
Fight rank 16 "Specialist", Split Rank 7 "Split Privileged Associate of Rhonkar"
Storyline
All

Note: You typically start in a split sector, and with low enough reputation for the split to shoot on site, and the paranid sending bounty hunters after you. You are flying a great ship with good weapons, however it lacks many basic upgrades like Fight command software, shields, and engine tunings, which make this a difficult scenario.
