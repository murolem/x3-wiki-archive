Overview

The Tyr is an extremely powerful Terran destroyer, and provides the heavy gun platform for the ATF. It has more weapon slots than any other vessel in the game, but since it can only mount Terran weapons, which are weaker than their Commonwealth counterparts, and also due to its inadequate cargobay it cannot have the powerful loadout that is expected from a ship with this accolade.
