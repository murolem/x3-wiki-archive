Overview

A M6 class ship with the turning capability of an M3. It can out manouver anything above M3 class ships. Very very hard to board. It is equipped with poor shielding, equal to that of the Dragon, and though it is not as fast its agility more than makes up for this. Its ingame role is to 'protect the large, slow and bulky Xenon Capital ships'. This it does admirably, its manouverability allowing it to chase down fighters that slip through the capitals' flak screens.

Combat

They can easily catch an opponent by surprise, and generally use the powerful Ion Shard Railgun as a main weapon, an accurate volley of which will quite effectively maim other standard M6 ships. M6+ and bigger ships, however, will shrug off the damage from a P's rather limited laser capacitor. Of course, they generally come in large swarms, so can put a pretty large dent in even a capital ship's shields.

Availability

The Xenon P can only be acquired by boarding.
