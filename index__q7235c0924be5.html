[edit] Overview

Considered one of the least desirable ships of the M6 class, the Boron Hydra is fast, with average shielding for its class. However, it costs a bomb, its weapon generator is outclassed by some M3s and its agility leaves much to be desired.

[edit] Background

[edit] Physical Characteristics

[edit] Combat

The Hydra is not particularly able in a combat role, due to its inability to sustain fire at its target. Its main use is as a missile platform or military cargo transport due to its large freight bay, which also makes it a capable user of ammunition-based weaponry, nullifying its generator problem somewhat. Like most Boron combat craft, its main ability is the use of the Ion Disruptor, making it very narrow in its unique role

[edit] Equipment

[edit] Availability

[edit] Suggested Roles

With a large cargobay and high speed, the Hydra is an effective armed freighter, as well as being an effective platform for deploying large amounts of ordnance such as missiles, drones, lasertowers and mines.
