The M7 is a Frigate class ship. Some of these can mount powerful capital weaponry such as the Photon Pulse Cannon, as well as anti-fighter Flak.

Most can mount the Incendiary Bomb Launcher, a weaker capital weapon.

Some are designed mainly to destroy fighters and escort larger ships, where as others can hold their own even against destroyers.

M7s are large, but not as big as an M1, M2 or TL. They are not as powerful as an M2, but are generally much faster with a similar weapons generator and layout as an M1.

Unlike an M1, M7s aren't designed to carry a large number of fighters. Many have hangar bays, but less than 10, with some having none. An exception to this is the Split Panther, a large and powerful M1/M7 cross with most of the stats of the Tiger but a hangar carrying more than 30 ships.


The M7 class includes;

  • Argon Cerberus
  • Boron Thresher
  • Paranid Deimos
  • Pirate Carrack
  • Split Tiger
  • Split Panther
  • Teladi Shrike
  • Terran Yokohama
  • Xenon Q
  • ATF Aegir

Note that the M7M missile frigates also appear with the designation 'M7', but are very different ships and are not listed here. They can be found on the strike frigate page.
