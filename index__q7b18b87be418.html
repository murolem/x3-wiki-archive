[edit] Overview

The Kyoto is an impressive and imposing addition in Albion Prelude, it takes everything that the Osaka can do and does it better albeit at a slower pace and a hefty price tag.

[edit] Background

Recognising the need for a more powerful capital ship that would not be controlled strictly by the AGI taskforce the united space command opted to develop their own super heavy destroyer to perform tasks that the ATF would not. The Kyoto is currently one of the most powerful ships in service with the Terran military.

[edit] Physical Characteristics

The Kyoto has a long hourglass shape from above and as with many Terran ships it has a very thin profile for it's size. Towards the aft the hull is tiered up to the bridge. The ship is two to three times longer than an Osaka and dwarves other ships of it's class.
