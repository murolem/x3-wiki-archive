Description[edit]

Encyclopedia Entry[edit]

When the Paranid first entered this system they found an empty sector accessible by two gates, but the planet remains a mystery here. There are a few remnants of a civilization with one relic - a needle-like object made from stone that stands 3 stories high, in the middle of what may have been a city. At the foot of the monument a large gemstone was found inside a carved stone case. After prolonged surveys it was discovered that there was one once a large building here, which was thought to be a temple. Scientists today are still trying to understand what the script on the needle object means.
