[edit] Overview

The Osaka is the USC's fleet battleship, and along with the ATF Tyr provides the platforms on which Earth mounts its heavy guns.

[edit] Physical characteristics

The Osaka has midrange speed for an M2, but has a higher turn rate than Commonwealth M2s giving it more manoeuvrability.

It is a very flat ship, making it easy to hit from above and below but quite hard to hit from the side.

[edit] Combat

The Osaka's shielding is the heaviest of all ships in the X-Universe at 12GJ. Along with the Tyr, it is the only ship that is guaranteed to survive the first volley of fire from a Xenon Q in OOS combat. This makes it an excellent choice for the role of penning up the Xenon in their home space.

It is also a potent ship in IS combat, however it has been shown that under AI control a bank of PSPs holds less destructive power than an equivalent bank of PPCs due to the PSP's low fire rate. Despite its weak firepower, its tough shields mean that it is just as likely to be left standing after a fight with another capital as any other M2.

It can equip SSCs in top and bottom turrets, giving it an impressive potency against fighters.

[edit] Availability

It can be bought with from all Terran shipyards with sufficient Terran rep, but due to the requirement for Terran weapons it is the most difficult M2 to equip after the Tyr.

[edit] Suggested roles

For any OOS combat role the Osaka is unmatched, but it particularly excells at anti-Q patrols in sectors bordering Xenon space.
