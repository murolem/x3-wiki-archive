The Shady Business Plotline is a mission added to Albion Prelude by the version 3.0 patch, continuing the main story.

Starting Conditions[edit]

  • Albion Prelude Main Plot or Loose Ends Complete.
  • Unfocused Jumpdrive
  • Highest Split Rank (Honored strong arm of Rhonkar)
