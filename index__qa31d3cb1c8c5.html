[edit] Overview

M7C Carriers are a a new class of fast and light escort carriers: Note that the M7C Class is only available in X3 Albion Prelude.

M7C are Less armored than there big brother counter parts the M1's in return though they have a higher speed.

[edit] Acquiring

M7C ships include:

Purchasable
  • Boron Guppy
  • Paranid Ariadne
  • Teladi Cormorant
  • Terran Maccana
