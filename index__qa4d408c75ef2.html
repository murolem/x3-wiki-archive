The M8 class ship is a small bomber. This ship stays far from the battle and fires very strong missiles to the target. Normally these ships are not well armed besides their missiles. Be careful firing missiles while this ship is under attack because the missiles can be stronger than the M8 ship's shields, and will explode if they are hit as you fire them!

All Commonwealth bombers use the Tomahawk Heavy Missile as their main armament; the Terran bombers use the Shadow.

M8 class ships include the following;

  • Argon Gladiator
  • Paranid Hades
  • Split Viper
  • Teladi Peregrine
  • Terran Claymore
