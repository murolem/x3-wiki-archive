Overview

There is little difference between the Mjollnir and the Sabre, excepting that the Mjollnir has more weapon hardpoints and increased cargo capacity.

Background

Physical Characteristics

Combat

Care must be taken with a Mjollnir filled with a full weapons bank, partly because such a ship represents a huge financial outlay and partly because its weapon generator is smaller than the Sabre's with nearly twice as many guns to power. Nevertheless, a lethal adversary in a fight, shielded and armed on the level with weaker M3s.

Equipment

Missiles
  • Poltergeist Missile

Availability

The Mjollnir is not available at any shipyard to buy, in common with all ATF ships. It is only available during the Balance of Power plotline.
