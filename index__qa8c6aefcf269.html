Overview

Not available in a Vanilla game, but present in the game files. This craft is canonically the flagship of the ATF, boasting large weapon arrays and massive shields. It is also one of the largest craft in the game, being too wide to fit through gates. Obviously unfinished, as there are also some turret placement and targeting bugs in its design.
