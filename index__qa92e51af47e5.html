M0 Battleship.

M0s are the heaviest ship class and are used only by Terran, Xenon and Kha'ak. A massive ship able to destroy fleets all by itself, with massive firepower at its command. After the xenon war they all surposedly were destroyed (Remains of a M0 exists in xenon sector 101 )
