[edit] Overview

The Kha'ak Corvette is not often seen outside Khaak sectors, though it does turn up particularly during the Final Fury plot. It is not an especially powerful ship and will fall relatively easily to another Corvette. However, it is still a foe to be wary of and like most Kha'ak ships, rarely travels alone.

[edit] Physical Characteristics

[edit] Combat

The Kha'ak Corvette is best attacked along its ecliptic plane from behind, where it has a blind spot. It has quite heavy shields as Corvettes go, but due to its overblown size it collects incoming fire and because of its inability to hit a shadowing target it is not a particularly potent warship.

It is a decent match for other corvettes that are not agile enough to remain in the blind spot, but not the heavy M6s it resembles more. Despite a colossal laser generator on the upper end of heavy M6 territory its Beta Kyon Emitters drain its power very quickly, leaving it with a fraction of its full power. Alone it is not much of a threat to anything with shielding in excess of 800MJ.

[edit] Equipment

[edit] Availability

The Kha'ak Corvette is unavailable in the vanilla game.
