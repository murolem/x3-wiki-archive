Overview

The final reward to Albion Prelude's plot, the Aamon Prototype is an extremely powerful heavy fighter.

Background

A prototype developed by the Argon Secret Service. It is a hybrid of Terran and Argon technology and although still in development, it is already very powerful.

Physical Characteristics

Combat

The Aamon is essentially an improved Notus Hauler, but with slightly better speed and handling, one additional 25 MJ shield, and higher laser capacity and recharge rate. The only other notable difference is that the Aamon can also mount Terran EMPCs and can launch the Terran Poltergeist and Spectre missiles.

It is one of the best M3+ craft available. It is capable of carrying typical Commonwealth weapons for its class as well as Terran ones, and features two turrets unlike most heavy fighter designs. Combined with strong overall stats, it is both a flexible and powerful design. In terms of raw power its only real competitor is the deadly Fenrir.

Equipment

Availability

The Aamon is only available as a prototype, and only attainable after finishing the Albion Prelude plot as a final reward.

Suggested Roles

An excellent playership, as only one is available. The heavy shielding and weaponry, combined with good speed and passable cargo, make it a very competent ship.
