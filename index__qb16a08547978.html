[edit] Overview

[edit] Encyclopedia Entry

This weapon forge manufactures the Incendiary Bomb Launcher.

[edit] Availability

Incendiary Bomb Launcher Forges are Pirate stations and therefore are unavailable for purchase.

They may occassionally be acquired by capturing TL Large Transport ships attempting to deploy them for the Pirates.
