[edit] Overview

The Terran equivalent of the Hammer Heavy Torpedo, this missile is specifically designed for salvo fire from Skirnirs. It has longer range and higher damage than its counterpart but has a lower refire rate.

[edit] Commentary

There is a big difference between the Shadow and its Commonwealth counterpart. The Shadow is usable only by the ATF Skirnir, and is even more of an 'I win' weapon than the Hammer Heavy Torpedo, with its frighteningly high damage.
