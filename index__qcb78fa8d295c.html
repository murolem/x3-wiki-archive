[edit] Overview

One of the most popular M7M craft, the Cobra is fast, powerful and sleek, a perfect strike craft for supporting or initiating attacks with its devastating missile barrages. With a smaller cargo bay than its fellow missile frigates, the Cobra's endurance is compromised, but mobility and versatility tend to be favoured over staying ability in this class of ship. With 3Gj of shielding it is unable to withstand determined anti capital fire and cannot afford to ignore fighters, but with retargeting swarms of the frighteningly effective Flail Barrage Missile, its fighter problems tend to evaporate.

[edit] Background

The Cobra missile frigate is an awe-inspiring and potent warship, giving an outstanding heavy strike capability to any fleet.

[edit] Physical Characteristics

Very small, very sleek. The Cobra appears to be armoured with Dragon hulls, and is an intimidating craft with the Split's trademark dusty red-coloured plating.

[edit] Combat

Not to be used in close combat, the Cobra is of great effect when handled correctly - as with all of its class, a well-stocked Cobra can devastate an entire sector without ever being touched due to its range. It is vulnerable to large fighter swarms and missile counterattacks, and its missile stocks should always be watched - no missiles make it largely defenceless.

The addition of a twin turret in the rear means that it does retain some native ability to swat fighters and incoming missiles.
