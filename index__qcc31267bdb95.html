Description[edit]

Founded to prevent the Boron expanding further in this area of space, this Split colony is heavily guarded and produces surveillance equipment and defensive weapons, allegedly for protection against Boron aggression. A covert Federation intelligence group in turn watch the Split for signs of aggression against the peace loving Boron.
