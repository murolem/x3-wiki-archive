[edit] Overview

The Split M4+, the Asp improves on the M4 Scorpion primarily in shield strength.

[edit] Background

The Asp is a highly maneuverable medium fighter, which can hold its own against most similar-sized opponents.

[edit] Physical Characteristics

Painted rusty red like all Split ships, the Asp resembles a crab's pincer, with two fuselages that meet at a combined cockpit/engine section at the stern.

[edit] Combat

Typical of Split ships, the Asp relies on its heavy firepower and evasive capabilities to defeat foes at the expense of shielding. The Asp's 25MJ is enough to take a few glancing blows from an M3, while giving it almost complete dominance over basic M4s and M5s.

[edit] Suggested Roles

The Asp is a good mid-range fighter to use on carriers due to its reasonable cost, heavy firepower and good evasion. Its PBE compatiblity makes it deadly against all classes of ship, while it costs a fraction the amount of an M3.
