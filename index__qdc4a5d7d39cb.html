The M3 class ships are fighters. They have stronger weapons but lower speed than M4/M5 ships.

If piloted by a good pilot they can take out most ships in the universe. For example M6 and even M7 ships if you are patient enough.

You can also purchase some M3+ ships.


The ships of the M3 class are;

  • Argon Nova
  • Boron Barracuda
  • Kha'ak Fighter
  • Paranid Perseus
  • Pirate Blastclaw
  • Split Mamba
  • Teladi Falcon
  • Terran Scimitar
  • Xenon L
  • Yaki Susanowa
  • Aldrin Spitfyre
  • ATF Thor
  • OTAS Venti
  • Terraformer #deca.cefa
