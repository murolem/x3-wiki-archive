Description

This sector has been recently identified as a Kha'ak sector. Although several patrols have successfully navigated this sector, entering this sector is not recommended without heavy backup.

This sector does not contain any jumpgates, but it is jumpdrive accessible once you have acquired its location. This sector, possibly like other ungated sectors, has the curious property of always being 12 jumps away. If you're feeling risky and have a huge jump planned, you might be able to save on energy costs by jumping directly to and from this sector.
