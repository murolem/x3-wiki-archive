Questline: X3:AP Quests


The Beryll requires you to help the Beryll syndicate retrieve materials to the Beryll Research Station.

Part 1:

200 x Computer Components

50 x Microchips

10 x Impulse Ray Emitters


Part 2:

Deliver a Xenon L to Beryll Research Station. (You must Claim from the enemy)


Completion Rewards

Part 1: 1,066,946 Cr

Part 2: 3 x #deca.cefa (M3)
