Capital ships are major investments.

Military ships are typically focused on destroying other capital ships, but their anti-fighter weapons arrays often make them daunting targets.

TL class freighters on the other hand represent the largest cargo bays available in the X universe and are the only means of transporting and deploying new space stations.

Because they are such big investments, they are protected by heavy shields.

Because they are so large, they may only dock in at a few stations and only in limited numbers.
