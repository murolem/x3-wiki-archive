[edit] Overview

The ATF Vidar is a strong candidate for the role of 'Best combat M6' due to its massive forward firepower. Its damage output is nearly unrivalled and its great agility allows it to bring its weaponry to bear with frightening speed and accuracy. Its shielding is on par with that of Heavy M6s, at 1Gj. However, its power comes at a cost: its range is heavily compromised if a full bank of M/AMLs is used due to slightly cramped cargo bay leaving little room for all of ammo, jump energy and missiles.


[edit] Background

The Vidar is an older ATF corvette, precursor of the Vali. It has been retired in favour of its generally more potent successor and will rarely be seen in active service.

[edit] Physical Characteristics

The Vidar looks Corvette-like, unlike the heavier Vali. A compact profile means it takes very few hits, and its reasonable turret coverage means it has good missile defences.

[edit] Combat

Utterly lethal in any combat role, under AI control the Vidar makes for a powerful patrol, but as a player ship it shreds anything up to and including destroyers with its massively powerful weaponry (potentially in the form of 10 Matter/Anti-Matter Launchers plus one of two dual turrets normally equipped with EMPCs) and great agility. Even large Xenon fleets can be bested with careful flying and a handy obstacle to hide behind. Tough shields leave a good margin for error, and its weaponry absolutely maims opposing ships of the same class and chews up anything larger. The only combat roles it doesn't excel at are support ones and missile lobbing.


[edit] Equipment

[edit] Availability

The Vidar is extremely rare in Vanilla, only witnessed twice in the game, both times during the main Terran plotline. The first time is when it is used for the player's boarding practice, though it is gifted to you whether you attempt to board it or not.

[edit] Suggested Roles

Blowing stuff up. Particularly corvettes and even lightly shielded frigates when not under player control. Under player control, the Vidar can destroy any opponent.
