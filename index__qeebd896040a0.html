[edit] Objective

Same as taxi mission but with greater numbers of passengers.

[edit] General Difficulty:

Easy/Medium, depending on circumstance.

[edit] What you will need:

  • A fast ship with size M cargo or bigger, the requisite cargo capacity, and Cargo Lifesupport System.
  • Docking Computer, to save time.

[edit] What you will find:

  • Anything up to 20 passengers to be transported across a couple of Sectors.

[edit] Commentary and Strategy

The passengers board automatically when you dock. Time is usually tight, so having and using a Docking Computer is a big, big help. A Jumpdrive will make this mission a cakewalk, but if you don't have one, you'll have to tear across space as fast as you can (something which the autopilot cannot do).

A TP-class ship is not a necessity, you simply need the requisite cargo space and the ability to get to the target in time.
