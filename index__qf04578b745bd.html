[edit] Overview

The Titan is generally a decent M2, capable of mounting most capital weaponry such as the IBL, PPC, IC and also CFA and FAA for fighter killing. The GC makes it powerful against other capitals. It may not have the most weapon energy or the best shields, but it is cheap and has no obvious weaknesses.

[edit] Background

[edit] Physical Characteristics

Like most Argon ships, it's relatively graceful. Long, thin, with a rather nice curved front giving it decent turret profiles and making it harder to hit from up, down, front or back, but making it a huge target from the sides.

[edit] Combat

The Titan is, like all Argon ships, the benchmark for its class. Average in every respect, whether it wins or loses depends on the relative strategies employed.
