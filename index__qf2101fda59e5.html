Description

The Split secured this mineral rich system from the Xenon rearguard early after the last Xenon conflict but sustained heavy losses doing so. It now exists as a welcome, if rough, trading post on a busy route between many species' systems. Few communities hate the Xenon more than the inhabitants of the system.

Shipyard Availability

Cho's Defeat shipyard is located on the border with Teladi interests.
It is in close proximity to the Split shipyards in Family Pride and Rhonkar's Might.
