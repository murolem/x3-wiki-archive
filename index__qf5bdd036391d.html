Overview

Large and with a good cargo capacity, the Pelican's weakness is it's snail-like speed.

Background

Physical Characteristics

The Pelican is derived from the Vulture.

Combat

The Pelican has high survivability, however it needs it as it is too slow to run away. The reduced number of hardpoints also reduce the offensive power of this ship slightly, although given the generally weak power of TM weapon generators this is not too much of a penalty in a long fight. The Pelican is capable of getting right into the middle of a dogfight and providing fire support wothout too much concern about its survival.

Equipment

Availability

Suggested Roles

This is the only TM that can be trusted in the middle of a fight; however, getting it to the fight is problematic. A good fighter tender, however its low speed are a severe penalty to any trading role.
