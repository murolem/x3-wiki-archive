[edit] Overview

An extremely dangerous Terraformer M3 fighter, its hull is very cheap, though not so for its weaponry. Lightly shielded but quite fast and lethal due to its ability to mount the energy drain-less corvette level weapon, the Prototype Matter/Antimatter Launcher. A full set of these makes the .cefa a very expensive asset to lose, but one of the most dangerous fighters in the game and fully capable of destroying most corvettes. It can only be purchased in Aldrin and its weapons can be very hard to stockpile without the use of an exploit.

[edit] Combat

The #deca.cefa's weapons array is capable of dealing serious injuries to Corvette-class ships, however it is not the most heavily shielded fighter and the Corvette poses almost as much risk to the .cefa. Should be used to perform a surgical strike against a medium-sized target, with plenty of distractions keeping the .cefa out of the enemy's target lock.

[edit] Availability

The Ship Production Bases in Aldrin produce this ship.
